Volkswagen is now presenting the new Tiguan EDITION 20. The anniversary model with R-Line exterior, a new colour and a host of exclusive details can be ordered from today, with prices starting from 48,180 euros (for 1.5 TSI with 110 kW/150 PS ). The company is therefore paying tribute to a vehicle that has shaped the SUV segment for two decades and contributed to the success of the Volkswagen brand.
