Volkswagen is celebrating the 50th anniversary of the GTI next year. An exclusive model is being launched in time for the anniversary: the Golf GTI EDITION 50 . With an output of 239 kW (325 PS) and a torque of 420 Nm, it is the most powerful production GTI to date.
