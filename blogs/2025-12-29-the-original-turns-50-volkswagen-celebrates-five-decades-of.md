It is the world’s most successful compact sports car: the Golf GTI . The original in its class. More than 2.5 million units have been produced to date. In 2026, the Golf GTI will turn 50 – and this will be celebrated with a variety of national and international classic events, the exclusive anniversary model Golf GTI EDITION 50 , and the world premiere of the first all-electric GTI .
